Mon Immersion

During my two-year work-study program at Enedis, I get the goal to create a website called "Mon Immersion".

The department where I did my work-study placement at Enedis in Paris hosts an average of 80 immersions every year.
An immersion is a one-day visit to one of the company's departments to discover the tools and activities involved, as part of a reorientation for a future job, or simply to discover the activity in question.

Mr AUGUSTE Williams, my N+1, came up with the idea of creating a website to facilitate the management of immersions in any of the company's departments by Regional Division (DR).
During my contract, I was asked to develop the site myself, from start to finish. After many months of development, the site was completed and the testing phase was launched in Quarter 1 of 2024.

A test version in user display can be accessed via this link:
